Hanbok is a traditional Korean clothing that dates back to approximately five thousand years
and encompasses the original culture of Korea. Nowadays, however, people hardly wear hanbok.
To encourage people to wear it, modern and casual designs have been added and incorporated.
This is the reason why the new brand, LEESLE, was born.
LEESLE comes from “Hwang Lee-sle,“ the name of a traditional Korean costume designer.
The clothes with hanbok motifs that she designs have advanced, sensational, and modern styles.
They also show Korea’s distinctive aesthetic sense, thus revealing its own uniqueness.
'An everyday style' that faithfully realizes the image of a traditional hanbok
and 'A han style' that mixes Korean aesthetics to shirts, jacket, and pants.
With these styles, you can comfortably mix-and-match a hanbok
with your everyday clothes that will suit your lifestyle.
LEESLE designs not only shows your everyday and travel style, they can also be used as special clothes
that are perfect for photo shoots, dates, small parties, festivals, and other special events.
Moreover, they can be used as formal clothes for weddings, first birthday parties, or concerts.

Everyday hanbok style: Chima , jeogori , and baji come in modern colors and motifs.

Han style: Motifs of traditional Korean costumes are applied to Western-style casual clothes.
