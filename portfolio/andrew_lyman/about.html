ABOUT ANDREW LYMAN

Artist
& Photographer

Andrew Lyman is a photographer and artist living and working in Savannah, Georgia. He has most recently exhibited at Non-Fiction gallery in Savannah, Georgia, and at Two Hustlers Gallery in Soho, New York. Lyman is driven to create and learn from his daily experiences and his immense group of inspired friends.

He makes most of his photos on impulse without any plan. He finds it rewarding to take photographs and finds something in them with hindsight. His 35mm point and shoot cameras get the most use in his work, but he is also experimenting with moving images.

Romantic
Stories

Three hours that slipped through our welcoming fingers like water.Water. Vital.They were vital hours that we wanted to hold onto but obligations made our fingers rigid and incapable of a seamless cup-shape.

Has water been so nourishing? A man would stray from a river but return when thirsty. The river could be an ocean it is so full. Reflecting the blue grey heavens above. Shimmering with the light that is almost impossible to see with definition.

Emanating a white that is beyond what I can see with my eyes.
